About Me

So you want to learn how to fly? I can teach you.

Jess

I am an aerialist that currently resides in Raleigh, NC. For those that are unfamiliar with the term aerialist, an aerialist is a person who performs aerial acrobatics sometimes with various apparatuses, such as long strands of fabric, trapeze, tightrope, metal hoops, etc.

I discovered aerial dance six years ago in Athens, GA, and later continued training under various artists in Wilmington, NC, Georgia, and RTP. I have performed at dance socials for Wilmington Latin Dance, on the USS North Carolina Battleship for 2015, and now at the Rickhouse for the Mambo Dinamico’s 5th annual showcase. I love to dance, perform, and share knowledge and talents with other people.

FAQ

Do you have questions about flying?

When should I pay?

How can I pay?

You can contact me through my social media or you can email me at comeflywithjess@gmail.com.

Where is it?

Carmen's Cuban Cafe 108 Factory Shops Rd Morrisville, NC.

How do I sign up/set up a class?

Send me a message via email, instragram, or facebook to setup and appointment. However, everyone must sign a waiver before flying with me.

What should I wear?

Please wear comfortable athletic clothing that is preferably tight with full coverage, such as leggings, spandex, under armour, etc.

When should I arrive?

Please arrive on time or a few minutes early. Important parts of the lesson are stretching and warming up. Pulling a muscle would not let you fly with me.

What should I expect during the lesson?

You should expect at least 15 minutes of stretching and warming up before any flying. After that, I will teach you how to climb, dance, split, pose, and tie knots in the air! The last 10 minutes are for a cool down.

Do I need prior experience for this class?

Nope! I teach beginners to intermediates. You don't have to know how to do a pull-up or climb a rope, and everyone starts from the ground to prevent injury.
